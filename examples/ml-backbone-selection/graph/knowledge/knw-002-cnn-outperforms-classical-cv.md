## Content

CNN-based approaches (specifically ResNet-18) consistently outperform traditional computer vision pipelines (HOG + SVM, template matching) for industrial surface defect detection. Our experiments show a 94.2% accuracy with ResNet-18 versus ~82% with the best classical baseline.
